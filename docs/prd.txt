Let’s start with a structured, professional draft. Here’s a complete **PRD outline and example content** tailored to your agency that provides **Design, Development, and Digital Marketing** services:

---

## **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

### Project: Digital Agency Website

### Version: 1.0

### Author: [Your Name]

### Date: [Insert Date]

---

### **1. Overview**

**Purpose:**
The purpose of this project is to design and develop a modern, conversion-focused website for a full-service digital agency offering **Design, Development, and Digital Marketing** solutions. The website will serve as the primary platform to attract leads, showcase portfolio work, and establish the agency’s credibility.

**Goals:**

* Communicate professionalism and creativity.
* Clearly present services and case studies.
* Capture leads through inquiry and consultation forms.
* Optimize for SEO, speed, and accessibility.
* Integrate analytics and CRM tools.

---

### **2. Target Audience**

* **Startups** seeking branding and website launch support.
* **SMBs** wanting digital growth through marketing and web revamps.
* **Corporate clients** needing long-term design, dev, and marketing partnerships.

**User Personas:**

1. *Startup Founder* — looking for an all-in-one partner.
2. *Marketing Manager* — needs web development and ad campaign support.
3. *Business Owner* — wants results-driven marketing and measurable ROI.

---

### **3. Scope**

**In-Scope:**

* Website design and development (desktop + mobile).
* Content creation (copywriting, visuals, portfolio case studies).
* Basic SEO setup and Google Analytics integration.
* Blog section with CMS support.
* Lead capture via forms (email integration).
* Integration with CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Pipedrive).

**Out of Scope (for MVP):**

* Client portal/dashboard.
* Automated proposal generation.
* E-commerce functionality.

---

### **4. Key Features**

| Category           | Feature                              | Description                                      | Priority |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------ | -------- |
| **Homepage**       | Hero section                         | Clear tagline, call-to-action (CTA), and visuals | High     |
|                    | Services overview                    | Quick navigation to each service                 | High     |
|                    | Testimonials                         | Social proof from past clients                   | Medium   |
| **Services Pages** | Design, Development, Marketing pages | Each page with process, benefits, and CTAs       | High     |
| **Portfolio**      | Case Studies                         | Visuals + problem/solution/results format        | High     |
| **About**          | Team and Mission                     | Build trust and brand identity                   | Medium   |
| **Blog**           | Insights & Guides                    | Content marketing and SEO                        | Medium   |
| **Contact**        | Inquiry Form                         | Capture leads and connect to CRM                 | High     |
| **Performance**    | SEO + Speed optimization             | Fast, mobile-friendly, structured data           | High     |
| **CMS**            | Content updates                      | Admin panel for non-technical staff              | High     |

---

### **5. Design Requirements**

* Modern, minimalistic, and bold visual style.
* Consistent color palette reflecting brand identity.
* Responsive design (mobile-first).
* Use of animation and interaction (Framer Motion, GSAP optional).
* Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA compliance).

**Inspiration:**

* Awwwards.com
* agency websites like: Huge Inc, Ueno, or Moburst.

---

### **6. Technical Requirements**

* **Framework:** Next.js or React (recommended for SEO + performance).
* **CMS:** Headless CMS (e.g., Sanity, Strapi, or WordPress REST API).
* **Hosting:** Vercel / Netlify / AWS.
* **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4, Meta Pixel.
* **SEO:** On-page optimization, meta tags, sitemap, schema markup.
* **Integrations:**

  * CRM: HubSpot / Pipedrive
  * Email: Mailchimp / Brevo
  * Chatbot (optional): Crisp / Intercom

---

### **7. Content Requirements**

* Tagline, About copy, service descriptions, case studies, and blog articles.
* Professional photography or stock images aligned with branding.
* Brand tone: Confident, creative, and growth-driven.

---

### **8. Success Metrics (KPIs)**

* Lead form submissions per month.
* Average session duration.
* Bounce rate under 40%.
* Ranking for target keywords.
* Conversion rate from traffic to leads.

---

### **9. Timeline & Milestones**

| Phase                       | Deliverables                      | Duration  |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------- | --------- |
| **1. Discovery & Strategy** | Requirements, sitemap, wireframes | 1–2 weeks |
| **2. Design**               | UI/UX mockups                     | 2 weeks   |
| **3. Development**          | Frontend, CMS setup, integrations | 3–4 weeks |
| **4. QA & Launch**          | Testing, performance optimization | 1 week    |

---

### **10. Risks & Assumptions**

* Delayed content or branding assets from client.
* Scope creep due to unclear feature requirements.
* Third-party integrations causing timeline shifts.

---

### **11. Future Enhancements**

* Client dashboard for campaign tracking.
* AI-powered lead qualification.
* Multilingual support.
